Before we can begin the experiment, we need to ask you a couple of quick questions regarding your self and your linguistic background.



Survey

What is/are your native language(s)?*

Which (if any) second-languages have you studied to an advanced level?**

How many years old are you?

What is your sex?

What is a good email to reach you at with follow-up information?




* Native means learned at home by listening to your immediate family speak.

** Second-language here means you had to take classes or lessons or get a book - unlike the native above, you didn't just find yourself speaking as a part of growing up. Advanced means you currently, or for an extended period of time in the past, used a language for conversation, either for work or socializing.
